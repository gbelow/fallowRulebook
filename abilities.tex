\begin{multicols}{2}

Skills can only be acquired once, unless stated otherwise in the description. When a technique is acquired multiple times, it receives an I, II, III next to its name, indicating how many times it has been chosen.

A character does not need to be at level x to gain the XP from level x. Some techniques only require XP at level x, but can be learned even if a character is at a lower proficiency level.

It is not possible to acquire a technique unless the requirements are met.

\subsection{Conviction of Adaptation}
\textbf{Worldview - Chaos - Introspective }

They believe that the universe is chaos and that those who do not adapt should be left behind. 

\textbf{Promoted Attitudes:} Learns to take any test in a risky manner. Gains 1XP each time they roll a 9 or 10 on a risky test. \\
\textbf{Prohibited Attitudes:} Loses 1 XP each time they fail or graze by on a test that is not risky. \\

 \convic{1}{ Can use adaptation XP as any type of XP considering the character's talent +1. When doing this, the XP also increases the progress of Adaptation.  }
 
 \subsection{ Conviction of Domination }
 \textbf{Worldview - Chaos - Extrospective }
 
 They believe that most people are mediocre and incapable of acting in their own benefit. This justifies their control over others.
 
 \textbf{Promoted Attitudes:} Gains 1 XP each time a social action or the effect of affliction or compulsion hits or crits. At most, once per turn.
\textbf{Prohibited Attitudes:} Loses 1 XP each time a social action, mental affliction or compulsion grazes by or fails. At most, once per turn.

\convic{1}{ Receives +1 in Deception and Persuasion.}

\convic{2}{ Can spend 2 AP as a reaction to a player's limit action to give them orders, which works like the suggestion spell. Do the same with one character of your choice if it reaches your limit action of Abuser. }

\convic{3}{ Any time you crit in any action against someone in combat or when they miss in one, you can mock them at the same time for 1 extra AP, which works as provocation and gets +2 to the provocation test.}

\convic{4}{Whenever an opponent uses suggestion or command and get a miss, they must make a will test against your DL for psychic spells to avoid losing 2 AP. }

\convic{5}{ Gets +1 to all tests against characters affected by any mind altering status effects. Also increases the DL of any spells used against such characters.}

\subsection{ Providentialism Conviction }
\textbf{Worldview - Order - Extrospective }

They believe and find comfort in the belief in a good universal order. If everyone acted correctly, according to the order, everything would work well.

\textbf{Promoted Attitudes:} Follow the moral code described in the skills.  
\textbf{Prohibited Attitudes:} The character loses 2 karma each time they lie. Loses 3 karma for allowing a creature with INT greater than or equal to 5 to be killed without granting the right to a trial. Self-defense or protection of the innocent does not count. Loses 6 karma if they unjustly kill with their own hands.

\convic{1}{Whenever they are about to kill a creature considered intelligent, they must grant it the right to a trial. If it truly agrees to follow the moral code, the character is prohibited from killing it, mutilating it, putting it in life-threatening situations, or allowing others to do so. Gains 2 XP each time they spare someone or a group of people.}

\convic{1}{Whenever they make a relevant promise and manage to fulfill it, they gain at least 1 XP. They can earn more for larger promises.}

\convic{1}{Whenever the character or someone on their team is lying, make a Will test DL 5. If they succeed or crit, they must rectify the lie (this does not mean they are obliged to expose the truth). If they do not lie, regardless of passing the test, they gain 1 XP.}

\convic{2}{Gains the ability to make any test safely and preferring numbers closer to 6 instead of 5 if the test is in line to complete a promise.}

\convic{3}{Gains +2 in social actions and mind altering spells whenever they are judging surrendered opponents.}

\convic{4}{Gives +Charisma/2 to the morale tests of the entire team when they say something encouraging. This can be done after another character fails the test.}

\convic{5}{When the character is trying to fulfill a promise and receives a wound, they can immediately perform an action surge, even if they have no STA.}
